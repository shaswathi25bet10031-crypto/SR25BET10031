# STUDENT GRADE MANAGEMENT 

## PROBLEM STATEMENT
The project covers the development of a system which includes adding student information, recording marks, automatically calculating grades, and updating records with options to view, update, or delete. It helps maintain accurate and organized information on student performance with an interface so simple and user-friendly.

## SOCPE OF THE PROJECT
1.Intended Users

2.Teachers who are looking to record and track student grades.

3.School and college staff members that manage student academic records.

4.Students who want to view and monitor their performance.

5.Small institutions looking for an easy-to-manage grade system.

## TARGET USERS
1.Teachers who need to record and manage student marks.

2.School and college staff who handle academic records.

3.Students who would like to check and follow their performance.

4.Small educational institutions that need an uncomplicated grading management system

## HIGH LEVEL FEATURES 
Student Information Management – Add and maintain basic information for each student.

Marks Entry – Quickly enter marks for each subject.

Automatic Grade Calculation – Calculate total, percentage, and grade automatically.

Record Management – View, edit, or remove any student’s information.

Data Storage – Store records securely using CSV/JSON/SQLite as you prefer.

Search Functionality – Quickly locate a student by roll number.

Performance Display – Display results in a neat and easily interpretable format.

Optional Graphs – Display performance in graphs .
